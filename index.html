Jimmy Zoppi

Summary

James Carter Cathcart, also known as Jimmy Zoppi (among other names), was a beloved voice actor of many characters, most known for his works in the Pokémon series.

Gentle Playful Loving

Fun Facts

  • He was born in West Long Branch, New Jersey on March 8, 1954, and sadly died on July 8, 2025 at the age of 71.

  • When he was in Highschool, he played in two bands, one of them being The Laughing Dogs, which released two albums on Columbia in 1979 and 1980.

  • He played bass, electric guitar, keyboards, and drums.

  • Some characters he has voiced are Professor Oak and Gary Oak (starting in Pokémon's Indigo League series in 1999), James and the Meowth of his and Jessie's ( starting in "Pokémon the series: Ruby annd Sapphire in 2003").

  • He had three children: Nicole Zoppi, Mackenzie, and Carter.

  • He was the longest-serving voice actor in the English Dub of .

  • The (selfish) Pokémon company also brought themselves to give their respects to him, through social media.

  • He was a guitarist and vocalist for the Carter Cathcart Band since 1981.

  • Jimmy Zoppi had many fake names for his work life.

  • Before his extensive anime work, he provided voiceovers for commercials, such as for Kit Kat and Coors Light, and voiced O.G. Readmore on the ABC Weekend Special.
